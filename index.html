Xounep Digital Ltd. is a provider of system administration, consulting, system monitoring and security services. The duties of a system administrator are wide-ranging: System administrators are usually charged with installing, supporting and maintaining servers or other computer systems, and planning for and responding to service outages and other problems.

In addition to system administration, we provide server farm architecture design, networking consulting and infrastructure rental. We also employ several security experts and can provide web application penetration tests based on OWASP testing guide and infrastructure test based on OSTMM methodology.

Our customers are usually larger IT companies that want to outsource parts of their technological portfolio and want to focus on their added value.
